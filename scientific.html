Red Panda Scientific Facts

The scientific name of Red Pandas is the Ailurus Fulgens. They are special animals that belong to a group called Ailuridae. Even though they have "panda" in their name, they are not closely related to giant pandas. Scientists put them in the Carnivora group because of some things they have in common with other carnivores, even though they mostly eat plants. Their wrist bone that works like a thumb, which helps them hold bamboo while eating.

Kingdom Animalia
Phylum Chordata
Class Mammalia
Order Carnivora

About Ailuridae:

Ailuridae is a special group of animals that includes red pandas. It's like a big family for red pandas where they all belong together. Just like you have a family with your parents, siblings, and maybe even pets, red pandas have their own special family called Ailuridae. It's a way to describe that they are all related to each other and have similar traits. So, when we say "Ailuridae animal," we mean an animal that is part of the red panda family!
